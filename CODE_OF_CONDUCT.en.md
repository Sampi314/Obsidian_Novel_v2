# 🤝 Code of Conduct

## 🌟 Project Commitment

The "Cố Nguyên" project is committed to building a professional, respectful, and healthy reference environment. Although this is a personal project and does not accept public contributions, we uphold high standards of ethics and conduct in all interactions between the User, AI agents, and visitors.

## 🏛️ Professional Standards of Conduct

These standards apply to all forms of exchange related to the project (including public discussions, if any, or interactions between users and AI during maintenance):

- **Absolute Respect**: Always use polite language and respect different creative viewpoints.
- **Healthy AI Interaction**: AI agents must be controlled to create content appropriate for societal ethical standards.
- **Data Privacy**: Do not disclose any personal information of the administrator or other users in Markdown files or Issues.
- **Constructive Criticism**: Accept feedback on world content (if any) objectively and politely.

## 🚫 Unacceptable Behavior

- Using offensive language, personal attacks, or political aggression.
- Harassment, threats, or behaviors harmful to the general work environment.
- Deliberately sabotaging data structures or introducing malicious content.
- Any ethical violation in using AI to create sensitive content.

## 🛠️ Enforcement Responsibilities

The project administrator has full authority to decide and implement necessary measures to maintain this code, including:
- Removing inappropriate content.
- Restricting access if harmful behavior is detected.
- Adjusting AI interaction processes to ensure safety.

## 📞 Reporting Violations

Any questions or reports of conduct violations can be sent directly to: **nkhoihue@gmail.com**. We are committed to maintaining the confidentiality of the reporter and handling the issue fairly.
